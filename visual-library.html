The Stack Exchange Visual Library is built upon the ideas established in the Visual Guide, which outlines the major goals and guidelines for Stack Exchange imagery. The immediate goals of this library are to serve as (1) a central repository for all hi-res imagery, accessible to the entire SE team (2) an entry point from which to work toward a singular visual style and tone, for the Design Team. We'll be building in resources over time, including tone, color, and execution guides.

Getting Started

All images are SVG, so they are hi-resolution and editable. If you have suggestions about how this library would be useful to you or your team, say somethin'.
