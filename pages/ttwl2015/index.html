Stack Overflow Careers

What is Tech Talent Week?

Tech Talent Week features a week of events which explore technical talent in London. Stack Overflow Careers has partnered with The Candidate Experience Awards, Talent Leaders Connnect, Women Who Code and #Tru London.

Event Schedule

16:00 – 18:00

For Employers

Stack Exchange, London EC1V 9BP

Stack Overflow Careers is hosting a panel to discuss Employer Branding for Technical Talent. On Tuesday 24th February; experts from Forward Partners, Nokia, BTMH Ltd and Zalando will share their experiences, and discuss Employer Branding Strategies and Tactics.
